IT Project

Overview

My project idea is eHUB; a website & APP that focuses on e-sport news and culture. Similar to sport apps such as Bleacher Report and ESPN, eHUB will include live news around the league and scores from your favourite team. In addition, to add to the cultural aspect, it will also feature trending twitch clips and channels in all relevant e-sport categories. eHUB will also feature original content such as podcasts, gaming tutorials, reviews and other entertainment content. eHUB aims to bridge the gap between e-sport and the rest of the world, by delivering trust- worthy and entertaining content.

Motivation

The e-sport industry is a marketed that is waiting to tapped to it's full potential E-sport traces back to 1972 when the first Space Invaders Championship was held. Fast forward to 2020, and e-sport has become a 1 billion dollar industry that is growing every year. According to Statista the total viewership of e-sport is 297 million frequent viewers with 347 million occasional viewers. With the increasing rise of streaming platforms such as Twitch, YouTube and Facebook gaming the total viewership is expected to rise to 646 million by 2023 (Activate Technology Media Outlook, 2021). There is an obvious market and demand for e-sports, and adopting a model similar to Bleacher Report, which thrived in the sports industry, may prove to be successful.

Description

Accesibility

- eHUB will deploy as a website and a downloadable APP on all platforms.

Live News Updating

- In any sport, people are wanting to know the latest news. - eHUB will provide up-to-date content on all teams or sport the user has subscribed too. - User's are prompted to select their favourite teams, players or other relevant areas. - These selections will determine what the user's newsfeed will be. - User's have the option to edit their newsfeed at any time. - Articles will be sourced from major journaling publications, while also featuring original eHUB content. - Based on what the user is clicking on and viewing, suggestions to other categories will be made to the user.

Live Scores /Fixtures

- Similar to the operation of news, scores will be available to the user for their relevant e-sport. - A user will have an option for a 'Non-spoiler mode' where the scores will be hidden to avoid spoiling the score for the user, in case they wish to watch the match at a later time. - Also including upcoming fixtures and their time (relevant to time zone).



Featured Twitch/ Facebook Gaming/ YouTube content

- Based on the user's selections, featured clips from popular streaming platforms will be shown on the newsfeed to create variety and entertainment. - Alerts can be turned on to let the user a know a certain streamer has started their stream. - User's can also subscribe to receive all the latest content from their favourite streamer.

Community

- User's will be able to comment on articles and communicate with other user's via comment sections. - User's will be given the opportunity to post match reactions where user's can share their thoughts and interact with other users. - Tickets to tournaments and merchandise to particular teams and players will be available for purchase via eHUB.

Original Content

Original content such as podcasts discussing current e-sport topics, articles analysing team performance and interviews with current pro-players will be available exclusively on eHUB

Tools and Technology

Firstly, Adobe XD should be used to create the overall design of the website and the app. Other design tools may be necessary such as Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator to create custom images. Secondly, This project will also require extensive programming. The website can be developed using web programming software and programming languages such as Django, Python, HTML and CSS. Other options include using Java, C++ , Flutter and Ruby. Looking at other popular websites and apps the most popular tools are Python, Java and Javascript. A reliable code editor must be used in order to write the code. Some options include Visual Studio Code, Intelli J and Android Studio. Due to VSC's flexible nature to program both APPs and websites, using VSC is a strong option. Moreover, database management software such as SQL is also required to keep track of user login credentials. Lastly and importantly, powerful OS systems such as Windows and Mac are required.

Skills Required

Skills required will be creative design skills to create a niche look for the website and the APP that is attractive to the market audience. Other skills required include strong programming skills in Python, Java and HTML. These skills are essential as this will be the whole service and product will be built by code. They will need knowledge in how to structure code efficiently, and able to troubleshoot any errors. Lastly, cyber risk analysing is another skilled required as the increase in cyber attacks on to developer and the user has been increasing over the years. Cyber risk analysing skills include; identifying phishing emails, or masquerading websites, ensuring all data and important files are secured and backed up.

Outcome

If the project is successful, there will be a great thriving community of e-sport fans, able to connect with each other and is able to receive information about their team any where that they are. eHUB would also become a world leader in e-sport news reporting and content, being an mediator between the e-sport world and the non e-sport fan. The impact of this service would be quite significant as it may contribute to higher e-sport growth and better interactivity between fans and their teams.
